WHO WE ARE

Attorney Profile

Fighting For You

Chantal has been a practicing attorney for two nearly two decades. She began her career at a national law firm and worked on complex litigation matters, including oil and gas litigation cases. She later opened her own practice with a commitment to provide exceptional legal representation to the most vulnerable — those who are facing criminal prosecution, or who are injured by the actions of others. Chantal has handled over a thousand cases, has had countless bench and jury trials, and has a high success rate in court. She takes pride in fighting for her clients.

Education

Chantal earned her bachelor's degree in Political Science from Queens College, graduating summa cum laude. She graduated from Howard University School of Law with honors in 1999.

Licensing

Chantal is licensed to practice in New York and Washington, D.C.
