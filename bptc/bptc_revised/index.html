ABOUT US

Located at the Brookville Park in Rosedale, New York, the Club operates year round and provides social and competitive tennis activities for its members. Brookville is a great club to meet other tennis enthusiasts and build your tennis game.

Participate in tournaments and events all year round. Summer is packed with friendly play, club tournaments and competition with other clubs around NYC. Winter has indoor tennis nights and tennis parties.

Club tennis professionals offer individual and group lessons for adults and young players
