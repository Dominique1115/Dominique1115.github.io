Tennis By Warren

Tennis Coach

My name is Warren Osborne and I have been playing and teaching tennis for over 27 years. I started out as a ball boy at the age of 13, back in Jamaica. By the age of 19, I was an assistant coach. In 2000 I came to the United States and adapted to the coaching system and style here. As I gained more experience at local tennis clubs and parks, I have had great success training people. It is gratifying to see the fruits of my labor as I have trained students who have received Tennis scholarships as well as some future stars.

Tennis uses the language of life. Advantage, service, fault, break, love -- the basic elements of tennis are those of everyday existence, because every match is a life in miniature. - Andre Agassi

Tennis is a sport that lasts a lifetime. I coach children and adults of all ages.
