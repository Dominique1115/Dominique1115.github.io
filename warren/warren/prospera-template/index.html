WhyTennis?

Tennis is a sport for all ages

Tennis is one of the few sports enjoyed by people of all ages. Tennis is a skill that lasts a lifetime.

Challenge yourself and compete

The mental and physical requirements of the game will have you challenging yourself with every match

Love and Addiction

Once you start to rally and play a few matches, you will develop a love and maybe even an addiction.

Learn to Succeed

Tennis will teach you to use your brain and a wide array of skills and shots to win. There will always be better players than you, but the fun thing about tennis is when you put the work in, improve your game, and move up in skill level. So get out there and improve your game.

Excercise

Tennis provides a full workout. It can burn about 600 calories per hour. Who said working out isn't fun?

Mental Fortitude

Tennis players have to learn to ignore the things they can’t control, and take command of the things they can control (strategy, performance, attitude).

Philosophy

"DISCIPLINE is the BRIDGE between your tennis goals and your tennis success"
